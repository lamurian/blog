Population and Parameters

We often regard the society we are living with as a population, but what is exactly defining a population? In a layman term, we may surmise population as all observable subjects inhabiting a certain location. In research context, this interpretation may not present as the least imperfect definition, but we can generalize the idea of “subjects” into entities of interest.
